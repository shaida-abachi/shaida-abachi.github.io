Design large-scale behavioral studies, controlled experiments, and surveys to evaluate perception, comfort, usability, and long-term wearability. University of California, Irvine

Conducted independent research on human perception, confidence, and decision-making using behavioral experiments, computational modeling, and neuroimaging. Built reproducible analysis pipelines for large-scale behavioral and fMRI datasets. Published and presented work on perceptual metacognition and confidence modeling.
